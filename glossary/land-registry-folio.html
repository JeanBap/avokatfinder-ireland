Land Registry Folio

The official record maintained by the Land Registry of Ireland for a specific property.

What Is Land Registry Folio?

A Land Registry Folio is the official document issued by the Land Registry of Ireland that contains all legal information about a specific property. It serves as proof of ownership and shows all mortgages, encumbrances, and other interests affecting the property. The folio is essential for property transactions and verification of title.

The Land Registry of Ireland maintains the folio system, which provides a centralized record of all registered properties. Each property has a unique folio number, and the folio is regularly updated as property interests change. The system provides transparency and facilitates property transactions.

Information on a Land Registry Folio includes the current owner's details, property description and boundaries, and all registered charges and encumbrances. This structured information makes it easy to understand the property's legal status. The folio is recognized as definitive evidence of ownership.

In property transactions, buyers require a certified copy of the folio to verify ownership and identify mortgages. Lenders also require folio information before approving mortgages. The folio provides the foundation for due diligence in real estate deals.

Obtaining a copy of the Land Registry Folio is straightforward and can be completed through the Land Registry office. Certified copies are issued promptly for property transactions. The information is public and accessible for anyone with a legitimate interest.

Key Facts

  • Official record of property ownership maintained by Land Registry
  • Shows owner details and all mortgages or encumbrances
  • Serves as definitive proof of legal title
  • Essential document for property sales and mortgages
  • Updated regularly as property interests change

Common Mistake

Relying on informal evidence of ownership instead of the official Land Registry Folio. The folio is the legal proof of title in Ireland.

Expert Tip

Request the Land Registry Folio early in the property purchase process. This allows time to identify and address any issues before closing.

FAQs

Is the Land Registry Folio public in Ireland?

Yes, the folio is a public record that anyone can access for legitimate purposes.

How do I obtain a copy of the Land Registry Folio?

Contact the Land Registry of Ireland or access their website for certified copies.

How recent should a folio copy be for property transactions?

A recent copy (within weeks) is preferred to ensure all recent transactions are recorded.
